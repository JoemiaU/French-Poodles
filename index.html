The Story Of French Poodles

Welcome to the story about French Poodles. Poodles are one of the smartest dog breeds.They have a curly coat of fur that comes in different types of colors and patterns. Poodles tend to live 10-18 years.They are kind,affectionate,calm, and playful.There are Standard Poodles,Miniature Poodles, and Toy Poodles.
